About Us

Born in Panruti,
Tamil Nadu's Cashew Heart

Panruti, located in Cuddalore district, is the undisputed cashew capital of India. For over three decades, our farmers have cultivated world-class cashews in the region's unique red laterite soil and tropical climate — conditions that produce cashews of unrivaled size and sweetness.

Sriram Traders was built to eliminate exploitative middlemen and ensure that farmers receive fair prices while buyers get the freshest, most genuine product directly from the source.

👨‍🌾

Direct Farmer Connection

500+ registered farmers sell directly to buyers, cutting out exploitative agents and improving farmer income by up to 30%.

✅

Admin-Verified Listings

Every product is reviewed and approved by our quality team before it is made available to buyers publicly.

📦

Vacuum-Sealed Freshness

Processed and packed within 24 hours of harvest for maximum freshness and shelf life, delivered anywhere in India.
